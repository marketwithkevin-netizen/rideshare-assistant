Codex Recommendations
---------------------
Consider making the app full-width on small screens instead of a floating card. The rounded white card is fine on desktop, but on mobile it uses precious space.
Add aria-live="polite" to the result area so screen readers announce the recommendation.
After tapping “Get Recommendation,” scroll the result into view. This matters on smaller phones.
Consider a sticky bottom action button if the form grows with fuel cost, mileage, weather, or queue inputs.
Use grouped sections if more inputs are added: location, vehicle, demand, cost/risk.
Make the result easier to scan: decision badge, estimated hourly, top 2 reasons, then expandable detail.
