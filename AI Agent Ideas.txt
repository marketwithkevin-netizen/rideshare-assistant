AI agents are basically AI systems that can:

* observe information,
* make decisions,
* take actions,
* and sometimes operate semi-autonomously toward a goal.

A normal chatbot mainly answers questions.

An agent goes further:

* it follows workflows,
* remembers context,
* interacts with tools,
* and performs tasks automatically.

Think of it like this:

| Type    | Example                                                                                       |
| ------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Chatbot | “What’s a good thumbnail title?”                                                              |
| Agent   | “Generate thumbnail ideas, create prompts, save them, schedule posts, and report CTR trends.” |

Right now, the market is shifting from:

* “AI that talks”
  to
* “AI that does work.”

That’s where opportunity exists.
